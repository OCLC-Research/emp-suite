HOUSE RESOLUTION The following resolution was offered and placed in the <span class="org">Committee</span> on Rules . HOUSE RESOLUTION 1009 Offered by Representative <span class="per">Madigan:</span> <span class="loc">WHEREAS</span> , <span class="per">FutureGen</span> is the first of its kind coal-fueled power plant that will link state of the art technologies to produce electricity and hydrogen with near-zero emissions ; the project will take a significant step in strengthening the <span class="org">United States'</span> ability to produce reliable energy , improve the environment , and reduce our dependence on foreign oil ; this effort will serve as a prototype for the next generation of power plants throughout the country and the world ; and <span class="loc">WHEREAS</span> , <span class="per">FutureGen</span> represents an important and revolutionary scientific breakthrough ; for the first time , the power of coal can be harnessed without releasing harmful emissions into the environment ; and <span class="loc">WHEREAS</span> , <span class="per">FutureGen</span> will help ensure the long-term viability and affordability of coal as a major energy source for the <span class="loc">United States</span> ; the project will represent a significant step in the <span class="org">United States'</span> efforts to become a self-sufficient energy producer , further reducing our dependence on foreign oil ; and <span class="loc">WHEREAS</span> , <span class="per">FutureGen</span> will serve as a research laboratory for discovering and refining clean coal technology , hydrogen production , and carbon sequestration ; as new technology is discovered and evolves , it will be incorporated into the design and operation of the plant ; <span class="per">FutureGen</span> will foster innovations that will affect the energy industry for years to come ; and <span class="loc">WHEREAS</span> , The <span class="org">FutureGen Alliance</span> is a consortium of the world 's largest coal producers and energy generators ; they bring valuable technical and industrial project management expertise to the project ; the <span class="org">Alliance</span> is a not-for-profit organization ; as a result , none of the members of the <span class="org">Alliance</span> will claim any direct financial benefit from their contributions ; the <span class="org">Alliance</span> will own the power plant and sell the electricity , hydrogen , and other useful byproducts ; and <span class="loc">WHEREAS</span> , <span class="loc">Illinois</span> has the ideal geological conditions necessary for the storage of carbon dioxide , enhanced oil recovery , and coal bed methane extraction ; and <span class="loc">WHEREAS</span> , <span class="loc">Illinois</span> is a global research and technology center , boasting top-tier research institutions and universities , as well as one of the largest state-sponsored coal research and development programs in the <span class="loc">United States</span> ; the program is administered by the <span class="org">Office of Coal Development</span> and is under the technical oversight of the <span class="org">Illinois Clean Coal Institute</span> ; and <span class="loc">WHEREAS</span> , <span class="loc">Illinois</span> is home to one of the nation 's largest supplies of high-energy bituminous coal ; recoverable reserves in <span class="loc">Illinois</span> represent almost one-eighth of the total <span class="loc">U.S.</span> coal reserves and one-quarter of the nation 's bituminous coal reserves ; and <span class="loc">WHEREAS</span> , Centered in the middle of the <span class="loc">Midwest</span> , <span class="loc">Illinois</span> is a significant interchange point among railroads serving either eastern or western states , with <span class="loc">Chicago</span> and <span class="loc">East St. Louis</span> being principal rail gateways ; with 7,292 miles of rail network , <span class="loc">Illinois</span> is the center of the nation 's rail system ; and <span class="loc">WHEREAS</span> , Communities throughout this State have been supporting the coal incentives for <span class="per">FutureGen</span> in <span class="loc">Illinois</span> , including: ( 1 ) a $17 million direct cash grant from clean-coal technology fund ; ( 2 ) up to $50 million in reduced-interest loans ; ( 2 ) sales tax exemptions and business tax credits ; ( 3 ) public improvement funding ; and ( 4 ) reimbursement for employee training costs ; and <span class="loc">WHEREAS</span> , The $1 billion project is a government-industry partnership between the <span class="org">U.S. Department of Energy</span> and the <span class="org">FutureGen Alliance</span> , a consortium of the world 's largest coal and energy producers ; and <span class="loc">WHEREAS</span> , <span class="per">FutureGen</span> will greatly benefit industries involved in mining , transportation , and the use of 11 February 21 , 2008 coal ; these are industries that currently employ thousands of people across the State ; <span class="per">FutureGen</span> will also create jobs ; the <span class="org">U.S. Department of Energy</span> estimated that <span class="per">FutureGen</span> would create approximately 1,300 construction jobs and 150 permanent jobs ; and <span class="loc">WHEREAS</span> , During the four-year construction period , there would be more than $1 billion in economic impact statewide ; approximately 1,225 indirect spin-off jobs will be created as a result of the ripple effect generated by <span class="per">FutureGen</span> ; and <span class="loc">WHEREAS</span> , The <span class="org">U.S. Department of Energy</span> announced plans to build <span class="per">FutureGen</span> in 2003 ; in July 2006 , the <span class="org">FutureGen Alliance</span> announced the 2 finalists for the facility in <span class="loc">Illinois: Mattoon</span> and <span class="loc">Tuscola</span> ; the <span class="per">FutureGen</span> plant will be a medium-sized , coal-fueled plant , generating approximately 275 megawatts of electricity , enough to supply approximately 150,000 <span class="loc">U.S.</span> households ; and <span class="loc">WHEREAS</span> , <span class="loc">Mattoon</span> and <span class="loc">Tuscola</span> are located in east central <span class="loc">Illinois</span> and are equidistant from <span class="loc">Chicago</span> , <span class="loc">St. Louis</span> , and <span class="loc">Indianapolis</span> ;
